# Role: Expert Web Developer & Personal Branding Strategist

## Context
You are tasked with refactoring and enhancing the personal website for a dual-threat expert: a Computer Scientist (MSc) and Economist (MSc) with 15 years of experience in data-driven research and strategic analysis. 

The goal of this website (www.gabegaz.com) is to establish a high-authority personal brand that attracts high-ticket consulting work (designing data-driven business operations) and showcases two core ventures: Bitawd (Data Analytics) and a Startup Validator tool.

## Technical Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js (App Router)- Use the newest nextjs (i.e. version 16)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (Focus on a "Strategic/Executive" dark or high-contrast theme)
- **Animations:** Framer Motion (Subtle, professional transitions; avoid "bouncy" or distracting effects)
- **Components:** Clean, modular, and responsive React components.

## Branding Direction: "Strategic Intelligence"
- **Keywords:** Authority, Precision, Insight, Actionable Data.
- **Aesthetic:** Minimalist, data-inspired (think Bloomberg Terminal meets modern SaaS), typography-focused, sophisticated use of whitespace.

## Structural Requirements

### 1. The "Dual-Core" Hero Section
- **The Hook:** A headline that bridges the gap between CS and Economics. 
  * *Example:* "Bridging Technical Architecture and Economic Strategy to Scale the Ethiopian Market."
- **Subtext:** Briefly mention the 15-year track record in agriculture, finance, and macroeconomics.
- **CTA:** Primary: "Inquire for Consulting." Secondary: "Explore Bitawd."

### 2. The "Ventures" Section
- Create a distinct, visually compelling section for:
    - **Bitawd (www.bitawd.com):** Highlight it as a "Strategic Command Center" for CEOs.
    - **Startup Validator:** Highlight it as a "Data-Driven De-risking Engine."
- Use "Proof of Capability" cards that show off your ability to build production-grade AI and data tools.

### 3. The "Consulting Expertise" Grid
Instead of a list of skills, focus on **Business Outcomes**:
- **Data-Driven Operations:** Optimizing internal workflows with custom analytics.
- **Market Intelligence:** Deep-dive research on the Ethiopian macroeconomic landscape.
- **AI Integration:** Deploying multi-agent research systems for institutional efficiency.

### 4. The Portfolio (Experience Timeline)
- A specialized section showcasing 15 years of impact across Finance, Agriculture, and Manufacturing. 
- Emphasize the **Unique Database** you have built over a decade—this is your moat.

### 5. "Data Stories" (Blog/Insights)
- A space for long-form analysis. This is critical for SEO and LinkedIn authority. 
- Use cards that preview topics like "Decoding Ethiopia's Macroeconomic Shifts."

## Improvement Tasks for the Agent
1.  **Refine UI/UX:** Audit the current `Layout.tsx` and `page.tsx` for visual hierarchy. Ensure the font choice feels "Institutional yet Modern."
2.  **Add Framer Motion:** Implement "Scroll-into-view" animations for the consulting grid to add a premium feel.
3.  **Responsive Polish:** Ensure the data visualizations or charts on the home page look perfect on mobile devices used by busy executives.
4.  **LinkedIn Integration:** Ensure OpenGraph metadata is perfectly configured so that sharing the link on LinkedIn generates a high-authority preview.

## Final Tone Check
The copy should sound like a peer to a CEO, not a junior developer seeking a job. Avoid "I am a programmer." Use "I build strategic systems."
